## 2. Press / source-request pitches

**Honest infrastructure note:** classic HARO is gone (shut down/rebranded by Cision in 2024, and its successor Connectively also closed). Working equivalents as of my last check — verify each still operates before relying on it: **Source of Sources (SOS)** (Peter Shankman's HARO revival), **Qwoted**, **Featured** (formerly Terkel), **Help a B2B Writer**. Set up profiles week 1; answer 2–3 relevant requests/wk (Claude drafts, founder approves).

### Ready-to-send angle 1 — the trend story

**Pitch:** "AI search is replacing Google for local business — and most owners have no idea what ChatGPT says about them."
**Hook:** By the time we've run a few thousand outreach scans (Sept), we publish the dataset: *"We checked N local businesses across ChatGPT, Gemini and Perplexity — X% never appear in any AI answer; in Y% of cases the engine confidently recommends a competitor."* ⚠️ Do NOT pitch numbers before the dataset exists — the stats asset ships as the blog post in content-engine.md queue #10, then gets pitched. Real data only.
**Targets:** local-news business desks, trade newsletters below, small-biz sections.

### Ready-to-send angle 2 — the money story

**Pitch:** "Local businesses pay agencies up to $24,000/yr for SEO — software now does it for $1,188. Here's the $22,800 math."
**Hook:** David-vs-Goliath pricing + the agency-accountability angle ("ask your agency what ChatGPT says about you — they can't answer"). Works for personal-finance-for-business-owners and trade media.

### Ready-to-send angle 3 — the tips roundup

**Pitch:** "5 free things any {dentist / contractor / law firm} can do this month to show up in AI answers" — fully useful without the product; alphaa is the byline, not the content. Evergreen; offer to every roundup/newsletter below.
